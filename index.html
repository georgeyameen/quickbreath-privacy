QuickBreath Privacy Statement

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Hey there!

I'm George Yameen, the creator of QuickBreath. I built this app to help people breathe better and find calm in their day. Here's the straight talk on how your data is handled.

The Short Version

QuickBreath collects absolutely no data. None. Zero. That's it. You can stop reading here if you want.

The Longer Version

QuickBreath is a breathing exercise and meditation app that runs entirely on your device. It makes no network calls, has no analytics, no tracking, no accounts, and no servers. Everything happens locally on your iPhone.

What We Don't Collect

To be crystal clear, QuickBreath does NOT collect:

  • Personal information of any kind
  • Usage analytics or app interactions
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Health or fitness data
  • Location data
  • Anything else — the app never connects to the internet

No Third-Party Services

QuickBreath uses zero third-party libraries, SDKs, or services. It is built entirely with standard Apple frameworks (SwiftUI, WidgetKit, AVFoundation). There are no analytics tools, no ad networks, and no external dependencies of any kind.

Kids Are Welcome

QuickBreath is safe for users of all ages. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there are no privacy concerns for children.

Changes to This Statement

If I ever update this privacy statement, I'll notify you through an app update and post the revised statement here.
